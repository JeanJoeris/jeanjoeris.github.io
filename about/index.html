About me

I am Jean Joeris. I live in Arvada, CO with my wife, a cat and a dog. I attended CU, Boulder and studied physics and math. With this as a starting point, I am taking my math and programming skills and applying them to web development. I am further growing these skills by attending Turing School of Software and Design I love to tinker with code, and in addition to web development I enjoy making games. I am also teaching myself about machine learning and neural nets.

This blog is intended as a place for sharing my successes, exploring computer science, and discussing game design. While it may veer into other territory, this is the intended scope.
